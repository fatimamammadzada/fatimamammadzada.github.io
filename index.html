About Me

With a Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering and current MBA studies in Project Management at Webster University, I offer both technical knowledge and strong project coordination skills that align well with the responsibilities of this role. Working in a lean team allowed me to take on diverse responsibilities early in my career, which helped me grow quickly and develop a strong foundation in construction documentation, technical problem-solving, and cross-disciplinary collaboration.
